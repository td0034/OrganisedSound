Please read the study information and provide consent to continue.

You will watch and rate a set of short audiovisual clips generated with an experimental instrument. Clips show the LED panel output with the instrument audio; no participants are shown.
Expect approximately — clips, each around 1–2 minutes. Clips appear in a random order. Please allow about 1–2 hours to complete all ratings.
Participation is voluntary. You can stop at any time. If you wish to withdraw your ratings, contact the researcher within 5 days.
Each clip uses 1–7 ratings and a few multiple-choice items:
  • I like this clip.
  • Coherence/legibility and novelty/interest.
  • Fusion, constructive/destructive interference, and overload.
  • Inferred structure/process and perceived human steering.
  • Which modality dominated your attention.
  • Which composition condition you think it came from.
  • Optional comments.
End of session: a short note on your favourite clip and how you think the system is operated.
